About the Conference

TechCon 2024 is the premier technology conference that brings together industry leaders, innovators, and enthusiasts to explore the latest advancements in technology. This year's conference will feature keynote speakers, panel discussions, and networking opportunities designed to inspire and educate.

Join us for a day filled with insightful talks, hands-on workshops, and the chance to connect with like-minded individuals passionate about technology.

Key Highlights

  • Keynote speeches from industry leaders
  • Workshops on emerging technologies
  • Networking sessions with professionals
  • Exhibitions showcasing the latest tech products

Notable Speakers from Previous Years

Grace Hopper

Grace Hopper was a trailblazer in computer science. She developed the first compiler for a programming language and was instrumental in the development of COBOL. Her work laid the foundation for modern software engineering.

Alan Turing

Alan Turing was a pioneer in theoretical computer science. He developed the concept of the Turing machine and helped break the Enigma code during World War II. His ideas underpin modern computing and AI research.

Ada Lovelace

Ada Lovelace is regarded as the first computer programmer. She wrote an algorithm intended for Charles Babbage's Analytical Engine in the 1800s, recognizing its potential beyond calculation.
